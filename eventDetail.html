AIT Music Production present OKEAN ELZY (UA) European premiere!

Sun 14 December 18.00

18:00-20:00 - Concert
20:00 - Photo with fans

Category: music

Okean Elzy is a Ukrainian rock band. It was formed in 1994 in Lviv, Ukraine. The band's vocalist and frontman is Svyatoslav Vakarchuk. In April 2007 Okean Elzy received FUZZ Magazine music awards for "Best rock act".
